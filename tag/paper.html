About Me:

I'm Tosin Animashaun, and in here is where I document stuff as I go.

I currently contibute to the technical efforts at TechAdvance, a software development outfit out of Nigeria that has got her feet in payments, aggregation, transportion and the digitisation of government services.

I have previously delved into entrepreneurship with a group of friends at Krohx and somewhat unofficially interned with NIIT.

Aside from my day job as a backend developer, I spend a lot of my non-work time garnering more general knowledge to fuel my autodidact persona. I like to read well written content, and I occasionally try my hands at crafting good stuff like I do with this space. Some other interests of mine include music, art, and psychology.

I am also an avid podcast listener. My listening areas of interest include: technology, psychology and history among others.

Being an autodidact, I do not possess a college degree -- I do not completely believe in its efficacy. I have so far failed two attempts to obtain at least one.

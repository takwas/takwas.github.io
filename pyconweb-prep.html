Come 26th May, 2019, I will be giving a talk on one of the beautifully created, nifty tools of the PoCoo team's arsenal: Werkzeug.

Werkeug, which I'd much rather refer to as the WSGI swiss-army knife, is one of those brilliant pieces of software that exist out there but gets little attention.

Armin Ronacher, whom I adore so much, created this in YEAR in a bid to XXX.

My talk is titled: Werkzeug: What's in it for me? You can pretty much think of it as an introduction to the good parts of the toolset Werkzeug. Yes, toolset, because it is more of a toolset than a tool.

If you have used Flask, then you have certainly used Werkzeug. But the distinction must be made that, while Flask is a framework, Werkzeug is a library.
